# 90-Day Adoption Roadmap (Condensed)

Week 1-2 — Foundations
- Kick-off training (2 hours)
- Identify 3-5 Champion Users
- Focus on ONE use case: Email drafting
- Daily practice: Each rep drafts 1 email with Gemini
- Daily 15-min huddle: Share results
- IT support hotline active

Success targets: 80% of reps try it; champions use it 5+ times; collect feedback

Week 3-4 — Expand & Refine
- Add Sheets analysis for Sales Ops
- Create weekly report template
- Start a shared "Prompt Library"
- 1-hour workshop: Prompting 101

Success targets: 60% active weekly users; weekly report time drops; 10+ prompts in library

Month 2 — Full Rollout
- Advanced training: Drive search, meeting prep
- Gemini Office Hours (2x/week)
- Pair skeptics with champions
- Start measuring time savings and gamify results

Success targets: 75% active users; avg 5 hrs saved per rep/week

Month 3 — Optimization & Scale
- Advanced workshops: complex prompting
- Integrate Gemini usage into KPIs
- Monthly AI ROI review with leadership
- Finalize governance policy

Success targets: 85% active users; 7+ hrs saved per rep/week; 5+ documented success stories
